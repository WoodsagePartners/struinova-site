## Guidelines for Use

**Client Confidentiality:**
- Strip all specific client names except Team Monocles (published case study)
- Keep organizational context generic: "A regional nonprofit," "A multi-team tech company," "Municipal government," etc.
- Preserve methodology, frameworks, timing, and outcomes

**Methodology Extraction:**
- Focus on process flow, tools, thinking approach, questions used
- Extract frameworks and structures (e.g., Simplexity 8 steps, Design Thinking 5 phases)
- Keep timing realistic (how long each phase, total duration)
- Highlight outcomes and capability transfer ("team keeps the skills")

**SEO Integration:**
- Map content to identified SEO gaps (see CONTENT_GAP_ANALYSIS document)
- Use competitor terminology where it matches your methodology
- Create both service pages (sales-focused) and blog posts (educational)
