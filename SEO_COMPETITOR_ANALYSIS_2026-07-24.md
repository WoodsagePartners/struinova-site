**The Opportunity**: Struinova occupies a unique white-space in the problem-solving/innovation consulting market that **none of its national or local competitors effectively own**: the combination of proven methodology (Simplexity/Basadur), real-world credibility (cold case work, 27-year operational history), and thought partnership positioning (resident facilitator model).

None combine methodology rigor + storytelling + high-stakes credibility.

**Quick Wins**: Target underserved keywords in Indianapolis + first principles thinking + problem-solving workshops (low competition, high intent).
